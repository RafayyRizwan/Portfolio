Site Report

I carefully considered several design components when creating my portfolio website in order to provide it a polished and expert look. I choose light blue and gray as my color palette in an attempt to create an eye-catching environment that would help me properly showcase my work. In order to improve user navigation and engagement, keyframe animations and grid layout approaches were used to create a dynamic and engaging experience.

Furthermore, I maintained more than 15 commits and integrated GitHub with 3 branches to provide strong version control. This not only highlights my ability to work well in a team environment but also my commitment to continuously improving and upgrading my project.

I used the Roboto typeface to bring a bit of elegance to the entire design while maintaining a modern and sophisticated style.Using principles from my beginning software engineering semester and modern portfolio websites, I painstakingly created wireframes to direct the development process and guarantee coherence and functioning all the way through.

Apart from exhibiting my work, I also included my résumé with ease and included connections to my social media accounts on GitHub, Instagram, LinkedIn, and other platforms. I improved the user experience by adding the "target blank" element, which made it possible for visitors to browse my portfolio and quickly access external sites without interfering with their surfing.

Ensuring a smooth user interface with a focus on simplicity of use and accessibility was my top objective during the development process. By including these components, my portfolio website provides a coherent and interesting experience for visitors while acting as a thorough depiction of my abilities and accomplishments.
